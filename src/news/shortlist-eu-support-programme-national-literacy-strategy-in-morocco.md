#### Project Title:

**_****Technical assistance for the European Union Support Programme related to national literacy strategy****_**

#### Location:

Morocco

#### Project Reference:

EuropeAid/140410/DH/SER/MA

#### **Contract Description:**

The contract consists of the provision of technical assistance aimed at operationalising the EU support programme for the national literacy strategy in Morocco, the main beneficiary of which is the National Agency for the Fight against Illiteracy (Agence nationale de lutte contre l'analphab√©tisme - ANLCA). In accordance with the objectives set by the framework law on education, by the ANLCA roadmap and by the EU support programme, a main technical assistance (deployed at the head offices of ANLCA in Rabat) and a non-main technical assistance (for specific activities, deployed within the six target regions of the support programme) are envisaged.
